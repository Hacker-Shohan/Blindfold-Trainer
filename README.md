# Blindfold Trainer

**Train your chess blindfold skills with over 1 million puzzles!** ♟️  

This is a **powerful chess blindfold trainer** built for chess enthusiasts of all levels. Solve puzzles, visualize positions in your mind, and improve your calculation and pattern recognition skills — all without relying on a board.  

---

## Features

- **1 Million+ Puzzles**: Practice with a huge variety of positions.
- **Difficulty Levels**: Easy (-200 rating), Normal (0), Hard (+400 rating) — choose based on your skill.
- **Rating Slider**: Filter puzzles according to your chess rating.
- **Pieces Slider**: Control the total pieces in the puzzle (or ignore pieces for full randomness).
- **Ignore Pieces Option**: Skip piece count for completely random positions.
- **Dark & Light Mode**: Switch themes to match your preference.
- **Responsive & Compact UI**: All controls in one panel — no scrolling required.
- **Clean, Chess-inspired Design**: Modern interface with chess vibe.
- **Instant Puzzle Generation**: Click “Get Puzzle” and start training immediately.
